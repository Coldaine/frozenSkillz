# Route: PLANS / RESULTS

**Fires when** an agent presents a plan before work, or a report after it ("I did X, Y, and Z").

**Primary surface:** plan versus actual.

**Read alongside:** techniques/evidence.md (mandatory; completion-claims section), techniques/visual-thesis.md.

> Considerations on this route are a working reading, not yet confirmed by Patrick. Confirm before treating as settled doctrine.

## Page archetypes

| Page | Question it answers |
|---|---|
| Plan vs actual | what was intended, what happened, what diverged |
| Artifacts produced | what exists now that did not before, with links |
| Decisions made | what was decided, on what basis, by whom or what |
| Blocked & pending | what is waiting, on whom, since when |
| Next | what happens without intervention, and what needs a call |

## Mandatory mechanics

- Planned / done / blocked / cut are **visually distinct states**, not four rows of the same treatment.
- The "now" boundary is explicit; anything to the right of it is intention, rendered distinctly from fact. Future work never renders as though it happened.
- **Every completion claim traces to an artifact**: a commit, a file, a test run, an output. A claim with no artifact renders as unverified, visibly. Assertion is not evidence.
- Every produced artifact is linked, not merely named.
- Decisions show their basis; a decision with no recorded reason is flagged as such.
- Items awaiting approval are surfaced prominently, with how long they have waited.

## Considerations

- Divergence is the interesting part. A report that shows only completed work has hidden its own information.
- Cut scope is reported, not silently dropped.
- Failure and rework belong in the report; negative results are content.

## Conclusion form

State of the work; what needs a decision; what happens next.

## Intermediates delta

The dossier is the artifact ledger: one entry per claim, with the artifact reference standing where a source would normally stand.

## Anti-patterns

- Prose bullets in chat, which is the failure this route exists to replace.
- A green wall where everything succeeded.
- Plans and results rendered identically.
